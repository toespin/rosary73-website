Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Rosary73

General Questions

What is Rosary73? ▼

Rosary73 is an interactive audio rosary app where 73 different people each contribute one prayer to create a complete rosary. It brings together believers from around the world in a unique communal prayer experience.

How does the app work? ▼

First, you record 7 essential prayers in your own voice. Then you can either start a new rosary with your prayer intention or join an existing one. Each rosary needs 73 participants. Once complete, you can listen to the entire rosary with all 73 unique voices.

Why 73 prayers? ▼

A complete traditional rosary consists of exactly 73 prayers: Opening prayers (6), Five decades (65), and Closing prayers (2). Each prayer in Rosary73 is recorded by a different person, creating a truly communal prayer experience.

Recording & Participation

Which prayers do I need to record? ▼

You need to record 7 prayers: The Apostles' Creed, Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, Fatima Prayer, Hail Holy Queen, and the Final Prayer. These recordings are saved in the cloud and can be used in multiple rosaries.

Can I re-record my prayers? ▼

Yes! You can re-record any of your prayers at any time. The new recording will replace the old one for future rosaries, but existing completed rosaries will keep the original recording.

How many rosaries can I participate in? ▼

You can participate in unlimited rosaries, but only once per rosary. Each rosary needs unique participants for all 73 prayer positions.

Groups & Subscription

What are private groups? ▼

Private groups allow families, parishes, or communities to pray together exclusively. Only invited members can participate in the group's rosaries. Groups need at least 75 active members to remain active.

How much does the subscription cost? ▼

After a 7-day free trial, pricing varies by region: Philippines: ₱25/month or ₱250/year; USA/Canada/Australia: $0.99/month or $9.99/year; EU/UK: €0.99/month or €9.99/year. Annual plans save you about 17%!

Can I gift a subscription to someone? ▼

Yes! You can purchase gift subscriptions for friends and family members. This is a great way to help others join the prayer community. Gift subscriptions are available in 1, 3, 6, and 12-month options.

Technical Questions

Which devices are supported? ▼

Rosary73 is available for iOS (iPhone and iPad) and Android devices. We're also working on a web version for desktop browsers. The app requires iOS 13+ or Android 7+.

Are my recordings backed up? ▼

Yes! All your recordings are automatically backed up to our secure cloud storage. You can switch devices or reinstall the app without losing your prayers. Your recordings are encrypted and private.

What languages are supported? ▼

Currently, we support prayers in English, Spanish, Filipino (Tagalog), and Latin. We're actively working on adding more languages including French, Portuguese, Italian, and German.

‚úö General Questions

What is Rosary73? ‚ñº

Rosary73 is an interactive audio rosary app where 73 different people each contribute one prayer to create a complete rosary. It brings together believers from around the world in a unique communal prayer experience honoring the Blessed Virgin Mary.

How does the app work? ‚ñº

First, you can choose to record 7 essential prayers in your own voice or participate silently. Then you can either start a new rosary with your prayer intention or join an existing one. Each rosary needs 73 participants. Once complete, you can listen to the entire rosary with all 73 unique voices.

Why 73 prayers? ‚ñº

A complete traditional rosary consists of exactly 73 prayers: Opening prayers (6), Five decades (65), and Closing prayers (2). Each prayer in Rosary73 is recorded by a different person, creating a truly communal prayer experience that honors Our Lady.

üîí Privacy & Security Questions

What if I don't want my voice or identity revealed? ‚ñº

We completely understand and respect your privacy concerns. For users who do not want their voices heard or identified for security or personal reasons, we offer several options:

  • Anonymous Username: Do not use a username that could identify your actual name
  • Private Email: Do not use your personal email address when signing up
  • Silent Participation: Do not record your prayers. You may participate in praying by just listening to finished rosaries. Your silent personal prayers are just as important and powerful.
  • Voice Synthesis: If you want to participate by lending your voice in rosaries or even start new rosaries, you may record your voice using voice synthesizers to maintain complete anonymity.
Remember: Whether you share your voice or pray silently, your participation brings grace through Our Lady's intercession.
Is my data secure? ‚ñº

Yes! All your recordings and personal information are encrypted and stored securely in the cloud. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your prayer intentions and recordings are treated with the utmost respect and confidentiality.

üéµ Recording & Participation

Which prayers do I need to record? ‚ñº

You need to record 7 prayers: The Apostles' Creed, Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, Fatima Prayer, Hail Holy Queen, and the Final Prayer. These recordings are saved in the cloud and can be used in multiple rosaries. However, recording is optional - you can participate silently as well.

Can I re-record my prayers? ‚ñº

Yes! You can re-record any of your prayers at any time. The new recording will replace the old one for future rosaries, but existing completed rosaries will keep the original recording.

How many rosaries can I participate in? ‚ñº

You can participate in unlimited rosaries, but only once per rosary. Each rosary needs unique participants for all 73 prayer positions.

Which devices are supported? ‚ñº

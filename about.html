About Rosary73

Our mission to unite believers worldwide through the power of communal prayer

Our Mission

Rosary73 was born from a simple yet profound vision: to harness the power of technology to bring believers together in prayer, transcending geographical boundaries and time zones. We believe that prayer is more powerful when we come together as a community, each voice contributing to a beautiful tapestry of faith.

Why 73 Prayers?

The traditional rosary consists of 73 individual prayers when prayed in its complete form. Each prayer in Rosary73 is recorded by a different person, symbolizing the unity of the global Catholic community. When you listen to a complete rosary, you're hearing 73 unique voices united in one purpose - prayer.

Our Vision

We envision a world where distance doesn't limit our ability to pray together. Through Rosary73, a grandmother in the Philippines can pray alongside her granddaughter in America. A parish community can maintain their prayer connection even when members move away. Religious communities can extend their prayer reach globally.

Technology Meets Tradition

While the rosary has been prayed for centuries, Rosary73 brings this ancient practice into the digital age without losing its sacred essence. We use cloud technology to store and share prayers, ensuring that your voice can be part of countless rosaries, touching lives around the world.
